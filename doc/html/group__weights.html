Allocate a new workspace for evaluation of interpolation weights. The workspace needs to be initialised with ::grbf_workspace_init_1d, grbf_workspace_init_2d, or grbf_workspace_init_3d, before being used to find weights.

Parameters
dimdimension of problem, 1, 2, or 3;
lenmaximum number of points per dimension;
Returns
newly allocated ::grbf_workspace_t.

Initialise a workspace for evaluation of Gaussian RBF weights on a regular two-dimensional grid. The th element of entry (i,j) in F is indexed as F[i*ldf+j*fstr+k], and similarly for wt.
